How to Play

Your goal is to survive as many waves as possible and move your way up the leaderboard by defending replit's headquarters from bugs, malicious users and repls, and more.

Place towers by opening up the tower menu at the bottom of the screen. Towers will upgrade automatically after getting a certain number of kills.

Typically, you can use the mouse, but there are some shortcut keys:

  • Arrow Keys : move map
  • Spacebar : dismiss messages/start wave
  • +/- : zoom
